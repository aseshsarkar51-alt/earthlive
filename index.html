About us

Some things deserve to be written down.

Earthlive brings back the slam book — the notebook that went around the classroom, the one you filled with questions for your friends and got back covered in their handwriting, inside jokes, and things they'd never say out loud.

Here, your page is your book. You choose the questions, send one link, and your people fill it with words you'll actually want to keep. You approve every entry before it appears, and you decide whether your page is open to the world or just for you.

How it works

Make your page and pick a question set — classic, deep, funny, or farewell. Share your link anywhere. Every signature waits for your approval before it's posted, and anyone can tap an entry to read the whole thing.

What we collect

When you create a page: your name, bio, profile and cover photos, chosen questions, optional social links, and your email or mobile number if you sign up. When you sign someone's page: your name, answers, message, and any photos you attach. We also collect basic technical data (device, browser, approximate location) to keep the service working and secure.

How we use it

To run Earthlive: showing your page to people you share it with, delivering signatures to page owners for approval, and keeping accounts secure. We do not sell your personal data. We do not show your private pages to anyone but you.

Who can see what

Public pages are visible to anyone with the link and may appear in search. Private pages show only your name, bio, and photos — the entries are visible only to you. Signatures never appear publicly until the page owner approves them.

Your choices

You can edit or delete your page, decline or remove any signature, switch between public and private at any time, and request deletion of your account and data by writing to privacy@earthlive.in. We will act on verified requests within 30 days.

Children

Earthlive is not intended for children under 13 (or the higher minimum age in your region). If you believe a child's data has been collected, contact us and we will remove it.
