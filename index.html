About us

Some things deserve to be written down.

EarthLive brings back the memory book — a digital space where your people can add their thoughts, memories, and heartfelt messages. No feed to scroll, no strangers, no noise. Just words worth keeping.

Here, your page is your book. You choose the questions, send one link, and your people fill it with words you'll actually want to keep. You approve every entry before it appears, and you decide whether your book is open to the world or just for you.

Your book

Make your book and pick a question set — classic, deep, funny, or farewell. Share your link anywhere. Every signature waits for your approval before it's posted, and each entry pins to a memory map, so you can see where your people wrote from.

Your diary

Not everything is meant to be read by others. Your diary is a private space that belongs to you alone — write freely, and nobody sees a word of it unless you decide otherwise. Each entry is private by default. When you want to, you can send one to a single person like a letter, or add it to your own book. Sharing is always a choice, never the default.

Your events

Reunions, farewells, weddings, birthdays. Create an event page and share a link or QR code with the people who were there. You approve who joins. Inside, members write about each other, add photos, tag friends, and pin memories to a shared map — a book the whole group fills together.

What we're not

There's no endless feed here, no follower count to chase, no algorithm deciding what you see. EarthLive is quiet on purpose. It's meant to be a place you visit, feel something, and close — not somewhere you get stuck.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

When you create a book: your name, bio, profile and cover photos, chosen questions, optional social links, and your email or mobile number if you sign up. When you sign someone's book: your name, answers, message, and any photos you attach. When you write in your diary: the text and title you write. When you create or join an event: the event details, your posts, photos, and comments. We also collect basic technical data (device, browser, approximate location) to keep the service working and secure.

How we use it

To run EarthLive: showing your book to people you share it with, delivering signatures to book owners for approval, keeping your diary private to you, and running event pages for their members. We do not sell your personal data. We do not show your private pages to anyone but you.

Who can see what

Public books are visible to anyone with the link and may appear in search. Private books show only your name, bio, and photos — the entries are visible only to you. Signatures never appear publicly until the book owner approves them.

Your diary is private by default and visible only to you. An entry becomes visible to someone else only when you explicitly choose it: sharing an entry with one person makes it readable by that person alone, and adding an entry to your book makes it visible to whoever can see your book. You can change an entry back to private at any time, which removes it from your book. Nobody at EarthLive reads your diary.

Event pages are visible only to approved members. The event host approves every person who joins, and can remove members or posts. People who aren't members see only the event name and member count.

Your choices

You can edit or delete your book, decline or remove any signature, delete any diary entry, leave or delete events, switch between public and private at any time, and request deletion of your account and data by writing to hello@earthlive.in. We will act on verified requests within 30 days.

Children

EarthLive is not intended for children under 13 (or the higher minimum age in your region). If you believe a child's data has been collected, contact us and we will remove it.
